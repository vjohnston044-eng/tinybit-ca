Hello, I'm Vicki

Small habits.
Honest results.

I spent years in a hospital lab watching what happens when health gets left too late. Tinybits is my answer to that: a place to share what I'm actually doing to feel strong and well, tested on myself first.

You'll find plant-forward recipes, simple training that fits real life, rest that isn't an afterthought, and a lot of encouragement to grow something and get outside. If it's here, I've tried it.
